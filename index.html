Prison Reach

"I was naked and you clothed Me; I was sick and you Visted Me; I was in prison and you came to Me." Matthew 25:36

There are fewer places darker than an African prison cell but it is there, in difficult living conditions, that men and women are very open to hearing the voice of God.Some people have to get to the bottom of the barrel so to speak, for God to get thier attention, and the bottom doesn't get much lower than a prison cell!

Prison Reach is working in over 50 correctional facilities in various countries around the world, conducting upto fifty evangelistic campaigns each year sharing the good news of salvation through Jesus Christ with those behind bars, and offering the hope of tru transaformation.

Join me and Prison Reach as I take you for an exclusive behind the scenes of an African Prison in Mocuba, Mozambique. This unscripted raw footage introduces to the dreadful conditions many of the prisoners face each day and how, in a simple way, God is touching lives through our programs of hope.

Lives are bieng changed, hope is bieng restored, and darkness is being replaced with the promise of the future.

"Assuredly, I say to you, inasmuch as you did it to one of the least of these My brethen, you did it to Me." Matthew 25:40
